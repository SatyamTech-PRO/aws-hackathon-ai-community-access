# Project Requirements

## Problem Statement
Communities and small businesses in low-connectivity environments often
struggle to access essential digital services, public information, and
educational resources. Most AI systems require stable internet and high
data usage, limiting their effectiveness in rural and underserved areas.

## Objective
To build a low-bandwidth, AI-powered conversational assistant that enables
users to access information and services through text or voice, even in
limited or unreliable network conditions.

## Key Features
- Low-bandwidth optimized AI chatbot
- Voice-first and text-based interaction
- Multilingual and local language support
- Access to civic, educational, and service-related information
- Simple and accessible user experience

## Target Users
- Rural and semi-urban communities
- Small business owners and sellers
- First-time digital users
- Public service beneficiaries

## Functional Requirements
- Process user queries efficiently with minimal data usage
- Provide accurate and relevant responses
- Support multilingual conversations
- Enable caching of common responses

## Non-Functional Requirements
- High availability
- Low latency in constrained networks
- Secure handling of user data
- Scalable cloud-based architecture

## Constraints
- Limited bandwidth availability
- Low-end user devices
- Time-bound hackathon development
